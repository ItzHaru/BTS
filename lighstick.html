BTS lightsick

BTS's official lightstick is called Army Bomb (아미밤).

Version 1

About two years after the debut of BTS, Big Hit Music (formely Big Hit Entertainment) announced the release of the group's fan official lightstick. On March 10, 2015, BTS's official light stick design was unveiled through the group's fan café. Having a translucent, rounded shape, this light stick mimics the pattern of a globe describing how the group has taken over the world with growing international popularity. All members also shared photos of them posing with the lighted light stick.
