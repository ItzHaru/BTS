BTS Fandom

BTS's official fandom name is ARMY or A.R.M.Y (Hangul: 아미). It was officially established on July 9, 2013 after the first recruitment closed.

Meaning

2013

"A.R.M.Y" stands for "Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth" and it carries quite some meaning behind it, given that "Army" is associated with the military, body armor, and how those two things are always together, the fandom name basically means that fans will always be together with BTS.

2017

BTS and A.R.M.Y are almost mirrored mages of each other and they have a relationship that is affected by each other. The symbol of A.R.M.Y is expressed as a door on their viewpoint who is waiting & welcoming BTS on the opposite door when BTS opens a new door.

Recruitment

1st Recruitment (2014)

The second recruitment opened on December 9, 2013 to December 31, 2013. The benefits of becoming an official member include:

  • A.R.M.Y membership card.
  • A.R.M.Y special goods (once per term).
  • A.R.M.Y special newsletter (planned to be sent out once per term).
  • Monthly A.R.M.Y events planned to be held.
  • Opportunity to a free invitation to  (once per term, advantage of first picks to choosing seats.
  • Official fan site A.R.M.Y level upgrade adjustments (authority to enter Present for ARMY board).
  • Advanced reservations to BTS concerts (after consultation with sponsors).
  • First dips for fan-club members during open broadcasting and events (after consultation with sponsors).
  • Chance to participate in open broadcasting participation events when new songs are released.
  • And more special advantages.

  • Official fan community online A.R.M.Y level upgrade.
  • Exclusive first pick reservations to BTS concerts.
  • Exclusive first pick reservations for fan club members to attend open broadcasting and events.

Trivia

  • In ARMYPEDIA, RM revealed that A.R.M.Y was only one of the choices for the fandom name. If A.R.M.Y didn't get selected as the official fandom name, fans might have been called "Bang-wools" or "Bells".
